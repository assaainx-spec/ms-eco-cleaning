About Us

A family business
with a passion for clean

M.S ECO Cleaning Service is a family business that combines cleaning with personal attention. With more than 12 years of experience, we know exactly what it takes to achieve a flawless result. We believe that a clean space starts with people who truly care — and that dedication is something we bring to every job.

We serve both businesses and private clients in the Westland region and surrounding area. From office to canteen, from home to restaurant — we work exclusively with eco-friendly products that are safe for people, animals and the environment.
